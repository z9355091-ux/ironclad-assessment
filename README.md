# Ironclad Assessment

Weekly analytical papers from Ironclad Intelligence on topics of geostrategic significance.

**Product:** Standalone, sourced analytical arguments — not summaries of daily events.  
**Target audience:** Senior decision-makers in defence, national security, foreign policy, and strategic industry.  
**Length:** 3,500–5,000 words per edition.

## Editions
- Edition 001 (in production): Australia's energy vulnerability and the Iran war

## Structure
Each edition lives in `editions/NNN/` with research, drafts, review, and final artifacts.
